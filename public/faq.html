FAQ

Q1: Is my vote private?

A: Yes, VotingApp uses encryption and edge computation to ensure your vote is completely confidential.

Q2: Can I vote from multiple devices?

A: You can access the platform from different devices, but only one vote per voter ID will be counted.

Q3: How do I verify my identity?

A: Our system uses facial recognition for secure and accurate voter verification.

Q4: When will the results be available?

A: Results are calculated in real-time and available immediately after the voting period ends.
